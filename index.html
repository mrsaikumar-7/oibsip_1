The moon: the eternal source of beauty

Her beauty radiates like the ethereal moonlight,
Casting a mesmerizing glow, enchanting and bright.
Her presence, gentle and serene as the lunar beams,
A celestial vision that illuminates my dreams.


Her eyes, like two radiant orbs in the night sky,
Reflecting the mystique of stars, twinkling nearby.
Her smile, radiant as the crescent moon's gentle curve,
Emanating grace and charm that all hearts deserve.


Her skin, luminous and flawless, like the moon's glow,
Soft and velvety, with a celestial inner flow.
Her hair, cascading in waves, like moonbeams cascade,
Gently caressing her shoulders, a celestial parade.


Her elegance and poise, a celestial ballet,
As she glides through life, lighting up the way.
Her aura, captivating, like the moon's enchanting spell,
Casting a captivating allure that words cannot tell.

She is the moonlight embodied in human form,
A celestial beauty that surpasses any norm.
Her grace and radiance, a celestial delight,
Forever captivating, like the moon's eternal light.
